Skills & Competencies

NEBOSH IGC

NEBOSH IGC is an internationally recognized health and safety qualification that provides strong knowledge of workplace safety standards, hazard control, risk management, and legal responsibilities.

Risk Assessment

Risk Assessment and Hazard Identification involves identifying workplace hazards, assessing risks, and implementing suitable control measures to prevent accidents and unsafe conditions.

Incident Investigation

Incident Investigation focuses on analyzing accidents and near misses, identifying root causes, and recommending corrective actions to prevent recurrence.

Permit to Work (PTW)

Permit to Work is a formal safety system for high-risk activities such as hot work, confined space, and work at height, ensuring hazards and controls are properly managed.

HSE Documentation

HSE documentation includes maintaining inspection records, toolbox talks, incident reports, and corrective action logs to ensure compliance and continuous safety improvement.
